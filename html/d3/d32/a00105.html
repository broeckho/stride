Detailed Description

The simulation runner drive simulator throufgh time steps.

It's functions are:

  • invokes the simulator builder (
    See also
    SimulatorBuilder)
  • manages elapsed time clock
  • manages time steps viewers of its events (
    See also
    sim_event::Id)
